Who are we?

Our motive is to keep all aucklander's Happy and Healthy. We provide Bicycle for Rents and also fix them.

Find us!!!

We are live in Glen Innes, New Market and Remuera.

What you can do with us ??

  • Rent a Bike and go for a ride as long you don't get lost.
  • We appreciate if you guy's to work with us. It's lovely if you want to.
  • You can donate your old one's, no matter how worse the condition is. We accept it.
  • We got different sizes of bicycles. We range from tricycles to gear, we appriciate bringing kids and join the rides. We do provide all necessary gears for your safety.
  • We fix your old one's with quality parts, Damaged bicycles which are beyond repair, we accept them for donation
  • Planning where to go? We are situated in the park, we got plenty of free space for your kids. For others who love to go on a hike we suggest you Mission Bay, Mount Wellington and Point England
